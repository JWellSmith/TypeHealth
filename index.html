Overview

TypeHealth is an interactive web platform dedicated to uncovering and addressing mental health disparities in marginalized communities. It integrates geospatial data and social media sentiment analytics to provide an evidence-based policy framework for healthcare organizations, government agencies, and community advocates.

🗽️ GeoInsights Map

Dynamic map showing sample mental health clinic distribution, high-risk areas, and disparity indices layered with demographic data.

💬 Sentiment Tracker

NLP-powered dashboard analyzing mental health sentiment trends on social media platforms like Twitter and Reddit.

  • Positive: 40%
  • Neutral: 30%
  • Negative: 30%

📊 Disparity Explorer

Filterable datasets (sample data) visualizing correlations between mental health outcomes and:

  • Race and Ethnicity
  • Income Levels
  • LGBTQ+ Identification
  • Healthcare Access
