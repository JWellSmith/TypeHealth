Our Mission

TypeHealth is a comprehensive interactive web platform dedicated to uncovering and addressing mental health disparities in marginalized communities. By integrating cutting-edge geospatial data analysis with social media sentiment analytics, we provide an evidence-based policy framework for healthcare organizations, government agencies, and community advocates.
